Our Mango Varieties

Alphonso (Hapus)

Season: March - June

The king of mangoes! Alphonso mangoes are premium, sweet, and aromatic. Rich golden color with smooth texture and absolutely delicious taste.

  • High sugar content
  • Aromatic fragrance
  • Perfect for eating fresh

Kesar

Season: May - July

Saffron-colored beauty! Kesar mangoes have a unique golden-orange hue with buttery texture. Sweet, juicy, and melt-in-mouth experience.

  • Vibrant golden color
  • Rich, buttery texture
  • Long shelf life

Dasheri

Season: June - August

Sweet and refreshing! Dasheri mangoes are light yellow in color with excellent taste. Great source of vitamin C and other nutrients.

  • Light yellow color
  • High in vitamin C
  • Highly fragrant

Local Varieties

Season: Throughout Season

Traditional Gujarati mango varieties with unique taste and seasonal availability. Perfect for making jams, pickles, and traditional dishes.

  • Authentic local taste
  • Budget-friendly
  • Ideal for cooking
