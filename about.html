Our Story

Keriwala from Girwala is a family-owned mango farm located in the lush agricultural region of Dhargani, Amreli District, Gujarat. With over three generations of farming expertise, we have perfected the art of growing premium, organic mangoes.

Our commitment to quality begins from the soil and extends to every single mango that leaves our farm. We believe in sustainable farming practices that not only produce exceptional mangoes but also preserve the natural environment for future generations.

Our Location

Dhargani, Amreli District, Gujarat

Situated in the heart of Gujarat's agricultural belt, Dhargani is one of the finest regions for mango cultivation. Our farm benefits from:

  • Ideal climate for mango growth
  • Rich, fertile soil
  • Adequate water resources
  • Years of agricultural tradition
